How It Works

Our Process

Step 01

Free On-Site Estimate

We come to you, assess your space, and provide a transparent no-obligation quote with zero hidden fees.

Step 02

Surface Prep & Repairs

Diamond grinding and crack repair ensures a perfect foundation and flawless adhesion.

Step 03

Flake or Metallic Application

Your chosen system is applied with precision — flake, metallic, quartz, or solid color.

Step 04

Top Coat & Walkthrough

A durable top coat seals the floor, followed by a detailed walkthrough to ensure your satisfaction.
