Frequently Asked Questions

What types of epoxy services do you offer?

We specialize in epoxy flake floors, metallic epoxy, quartz blends, solid color systems, and epoxy countertops for both residential and commercial properties.

Most epoxy flooring projects are completed in 1 to 3 days depending on size, prep needs, and chosen finish.

Yes. All of our floors include a workmanship guarantee and material warranties where applicable. We stand behind our work.
